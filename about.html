About SJCIT

SJC Institute of Technology (SJCIT) is an engineering and management college in Chikkaballapur, Karnataka, established in 1986 under the Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust. It is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), accredited by the AICTE, and has received NAAC accreditation. The institute offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses, emphasizing a blend of academics, industry skills, and extracurricular activities on a 64-acre campus.

SJCIT offers various engineering and management programs, including undergraduate degrees in Computer Science, Mechanical, Civil, Aeronautical, and other fields, as well as postgraduate degrees and an MBA. The college is affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU). The institute is accredited by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) and recognized by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE).
